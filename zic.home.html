KNOW YOUR TRIGGERS

The addiction of smoking cigarettes is both physical and mental, a craving can consist of physical and emotional temptations. Remember, some of your cravings are triggered on a subconscious level, in other words...Your brain wanting you to fulfill an emotional expectation. Focusing on that fulfillment can put you into a trance- like state of mind. For example, You just had a huge argument with your spouse and you need a cigarette, it helps alleviate that negative emotion you are experiencing. When in this state of fixation logical reasoning has little impact to decision making. Over the years a hardened association between lighting a cigarette and managing certain feelings are developed. That's why it's so difficult to quit mentally.
